History in the Details

Stonecutters were paid by the piece. To ensure they got their due, they carved a personal symbol into every block they finished. These marks are personal, geometrical, and infinitely varied.

Preservation

Fighting Time

Weather, pollution, and renovation are slowly erasing these marks. By creating a digital twin of every mark found, we ensure that even if the stone crumbles, the mason's signature remains.
