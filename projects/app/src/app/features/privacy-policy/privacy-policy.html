Privacy Policy

We protect your personal data

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, for what purposes, on what legal basis (GDPR), and for how long we retain it, as well as your rights as a data subject.

Data Collected

Account data, platform interactions, and submitted content (e.g., photos and respective metadata).

Purposes

Account management, service provision, application improvement, and compliance with legal obligations.

GDPR & Rights

Processing based on Article 6 of the GDPR. Ensures access, rectification, erasure, and other legal guarantees for data subjects.

1. What personal data do we collect?

In the context of using the application, we may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Identification data: name, email address, and, if applicable, profile photo.
  • Usage data: actions performed in the application (e.g., content creation, favorites, searches made).
  • Submitted content: photos, descriptions, and associated technical metadata (such as capture date/time and, if authorized, approximate location).
  • Technical data: basic device and browser information, necessary to ensure the platform’s security and proper functioning.

2. Purposes of processing and legal basis (GDPR)

Personal data is processed for specific, explicit, and legitimate purposes, in accordance with Article 5 of the GDPR. In particular:

  • Account management and authentication: to allow registration, login, and continuous use of the application. (Legal basis: Article 6(1)(b) – performance of a contract.)
  • Management of submitted content: to receive, store, and display photos and associated information, as well as manage the submission history. (Legal basis: Article 6(1)(b) – performance of a contract.)
  • Platform improvement and security: to monitor performance, prevent abuse, and improve features. (Legal basis: Article 6(1)(f) – legitimate interest.)
  • Compliance with legal obligations: to respond to requests from competent authorities or comply with applicable legal requirements. (Legal basis: Article 6(1)(c) – compliance with a legal obligation.)

3. Data retention period

Personal data is retained only for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, in accordance with the storage limitation principle (Article 5(1)(e) of the GDPR):

  • Account data: kept while the account is active or until a deletion request is made.
  • Submitted content: kept as long as it is relevant for the use of the application or until a deletion request by the user.
  • Technical and security logs: retained for shorter periods, strictly necessary for auditing and security.

4. Sharing of personal data

We do not sell or commercialize your personal data. Data sharing is limited to the following cases:

  • Service providers strictly necessary for the technical operation of the platform, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • When required by law or by a competent authority, under European Union or Member State law.
  • With your explicit consent, if applicable, for additional purposes clearly identified.

5. Data subject rights

Under Articles 12 to 22 of the GDPR, users have, among others, the following rights:

  • Right of access: to obtain confirmation as to whether your data is being processed and to access that information.
  • Right to rectification: to request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: to request the deletion of data when legal requirements are met.
  • Right to restriction of processing: to request the temporary suspension of certain processing operations.
  • Right to object: to object to processing based on legitimate interest, for reasons related to your particular situation.
  • Right to data portability: to receive the data you have provided to us in a structured format, or request its transmission to another controller.

To exercise any of these rights, you may contact us through the means indicated in the following section.

6. Data security and contacts

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or improper alteration.

As this is an academic project, any additional questions about privacy or requests regarding your data can be addressed to the team responsible for the application.
